About Escapism

Escapism is a photography portfolio website created by Odysseas Ampatzis. It showcases a collection of his best work, including landscapes, portraits, and urban photography. The website also features a client commissions system, allowing visitors to request custom photography services. With a focus on creativity and visual storytelling, Escapism aims to inspire and captivate its audience through stunning imagery.

Purpose

The primary purpose of Escapism is to provide a platform for Odysseas Ampatzis to share his photography with the world. It serves as a portfolio to attract potential clients and collaborators, while also offering a space for art enthusiasts to explore and appreciate his work. Additionally, the website aims to foster a community of photography lovers and provide resources for aspiring photographers.

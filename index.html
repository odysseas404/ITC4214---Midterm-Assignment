Welcome to Escapism

Escapism is a photography portfolio, designed to display the work of Odysseas Ampatzis, a passionate photographer from Greece. Having started experimenting with photography in 2019 as a film studies student, Odysseas has developed a unique style that captures the essence of his travels and experiences.
